Welcome to your luxurious home away from home

Villa Waha, located in Berawa on Bali’s Southwestern Coastline,
offers a serene and comfortable getaway surrounded by rice fields.
The villa features a blend of open-air living and air-conditioned
private spaces, creating a contemporary atmosphere that
harmonizes Eastern and Western design elements, perfect for
relaxation.

Location

Villa Waha’s strategic location provides easy access to various popular destinations in Bali. Nestled in a prime location, in Berawa on Bali’s south-western coast, offering a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle.


Ngurah Rai International Airport is conveniently located just 45 minutes away, ensuring a smooth transition to and from your luxurious holiday villa.

Seminyak is just a 15-minute drive away, providing a plethora of dining, shopping, and entertainment options.

Canggu known for its laid-back surf vibes, dining and shopping, is a quick 10-minute drive from the villa.


Ubud is reachable in 60 minutes for a cultural escape, offering a tranquil retreat amidst lush rice terraces and traditional Balinese arts.
