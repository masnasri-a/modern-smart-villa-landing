Exclusive Facilities

Elegant Coastal Stone & Concrete Bathroom Retreat

Our elegant coastal stone and concrete bathroom retreat offers a serene escape. With natural materials and a soothing color palette, this space invites relaxation. Enjoy a deep soak in the freestanding tub or rejuvenate in the spacious walk-in shower, all while surrounded by the beauty of nature.

Cozy Private Pool

Immerse yourself in luxury with our cozy private pool. Enjoy a refreshing swim in the pristine, beautifully landscaped pool, or sink into the indoor jacuzzi for a cozy soak. For an outdoor retreat, our state-of-the-art outdoor jacuzzi offers the perfect spot to unwind under the Balinese sky.

Modern Tropical Master Bedroom Retreat

Experience the ultimate in relaxation and luxury in our modern tropical master bedroom retreat. This spacious sanctuary features a king-sized bed with premium linens, a private balcony with stunning views, and an en-suite bathroom with a soaking tub and rain shower. Immerse yourself in the tranquil ambiance, surrounded by natural materials and lush greenery.
